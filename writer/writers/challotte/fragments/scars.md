# Scars

Tracked,
through the wound to remind me what i've seen.

There..
always hidden. 
A part always beaten.

I've seen the best, worst 
Not only them all, but all sometimes i couldnt show.

I've tried hidding it,
it gets worse everytime i do so.

They said it needs the wind to brush
eat, run through them trying to dry all that moisture 
Moisture that servers as reminder of how painful it all was.

Scars i love that you are there and don't want you to fade.

I am afraid.. Soo scared..

what if someday you heal?
evapourate and i could nor longer feel you?

how will i remember, remember all that you came by with?
