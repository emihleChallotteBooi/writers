About the project

A private notebook made public with care.

Subconscious Prints is a two-writer archive for stories, thoughts, fragments, and reflections that come from the places people often keep hidden.

It is not built like a public forum or social platform. Readers do not need accounts. Comments, likes, and noisy feeds are intentionally absent. The focus is the writing, the mood, and the quiet act of reading.

This first version is static: posts and fragments live as Markdown files inside each writer’s folder. Later, the same structure can connect to a CMS or database without changing the emotional direction of the website.
