# You do not love me

You do not love me.

You do not even know me, let alone try to.
You have no intention of seeing me beyond the surface.

You plan not to tour the scars,
the lies
those that lie beneath the surface you experience first.

You want the lie,
spot the lie,
quickly construct a mere conclusion
of that which you have not known.

You do not love me.

You have no desire to give more of your time
to see me through my own eyes,
right through the depths and hopes of my soul.
You have no desire to stare past my siren eyes,
see no lust,
no sexual nature,
but more of what constructs them like so.

You want surface.

Transaction.

A barren bowl,
that which could hold your experiences,
remember all about you like memory
except it is in human form,
very warm,
and could fulfill the things you cannot.

You do not love me.

You do not want to love me.
You have no desire to learn to want me.
You do not yearn to know me past this horizon.

You just want a companion
to carry your cross with.

You want

not to die of cold in winter,
not to die of ill-prepared hunger combats.

You want a business-like partnership from me.

You say,
I will do this,
and you do that,
like that is all it should ever be.

If you loved me,
I would know.

You would hold me
like you were trying to learn me,
studying an ancient book
you have been yearning to digest for decades.
The ins and outs of my breath
would be elements that excite you.
You would gaze not into my eyes,
but straight to my depths.
Deep,
deep,
deep
way beyond those blooded veins.

You would see that window to the soul,
carefully constructed as siren eyes.
Even when you do get swept off your feet,
deeply empowered by sexual desires,
you would do so to me
as you would wish to do internal.

If you loved me,
you would see me as more than just,
and more of all.
You would want to do for me.
Learn my paths.
Trace my content
from origin to now.
You would not want to be good with me,
but exceptional to me.

Memorize my ways of living
so you know me beneath and above.
You would want to learn my impulse,
what I possess
when possessed alone.

You would want to know me
just like I think I know me.

You would want to understand
the dilation of my pupils
to know when I am taken aback in awe,
and when I am in danger.

You would carefully construct
your spirit animal to align with mine,
or mine with yours,
to not only wish to protect me in flesh,
but also in there—
that which we still yearn
to fully experience.

You would want to intertwine with me,
not only in flesh,
but more so in the highest dimensions,
higher planes,
higher consciousness.

You would claim me
before this world
and the next.
