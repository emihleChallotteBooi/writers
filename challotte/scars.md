# Scars

scars, tracked through the wound only to remind me what i've seen.

there, always hidden a part always beaten.

i've seen the best, worst and not only them all but all sometimes i couldnt show.

i've tried hidding it but it gets worse everytime i do so.

they said they need the wind to brush, eat and run through them trying to dry all that moisture reminder of how painful it all was.

scars i love that you are there and don't want you to fade.

im afraid..

what if someday you heal and heal then get evapourated and i could nor longer feel you?

how will i remember, remember all that you came by with
